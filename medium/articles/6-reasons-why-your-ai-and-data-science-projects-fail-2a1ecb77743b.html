6 Reasons Why Your AI And Data Science Projects Fail

Analyzing the 6 main reasons leading to the failure of your AI and Data Science projects

There are tons of wonderful AI and Data Science projects that are continuously getting developed in recent years. From innovative works of OpenAI, masterclass chatbots, facial image generations from GANs, and so much more.

As we continue to advance and progress in these fields, there will be tons more awesome projects that will make their way through the development phase and reach the general audience to explore and be fascinated about.

For someone who is getting started with these subjects, it is crucial to understand that Data Science and AI projects can sometimes be more complicated than we imagine. As you climb up the skill ceiling, there are tons of noteworthy points to keep in mind as you work on new ventures.

While you might be successful in your initial endeavors on beginner-level projects, the increasing complexity of more advanced-level projects could cause several hindrances when you work on them. It is paramount to understand the reasons for the failure of these Data Science and AI projects.

We will discuss six of the primary reasons due to which your Data Science and AI projects are most likely to fail. We will analyze each of these points in-depth and try to figure the most optimal solutions in which the following reasons for failure are avoidable.

1. Lack Of Data:

The significance of data to solve any kind of AI or Data Science project is paramount.

For every project, the need for well-interpreted data is a must. Without the right quantity of quality data, it is nearly impossible to train any type of machine learning or deep learning model to achieve the most suitable results that a data scientist might look for.

During the collection of data, you need to process the quality of the data that you are utilizing for the particular project accordingly. For complex medical segmentation tasks or other similar fields where you possess lesser data, it becomes paramount to adapt and optimize to achieve better results.

The method of obtaining the data for natural language processing tasks and computer vision programs could be slightly different depending on the type of problem that you are trying to solve.

However, the critical point to note for either type of problem is to ensure that the data meets the requirement and you have sufficient amounts to create your desired projects. There are several methods of obtaining the right form of data for a particular task.

One of the more prominent techniques for creating more data from existing data is with the help of data augmentation for computer vision tasks. You can perform numerous actions such as horizontal flips, vertical flips, optical distortion, resizing, and so much more.

2. Improper Sequential Order:

When cooking a meal, it is usually a good idea to follow a recipe and the sequential order in which some executive steps must be performed. Mixing up the procedure and the way certain ingredients must be added can lead to a disaster.

A similar scenario applies to Data Science and AI as well, where you cannot afford to mix up the essential ingredients as you desire. Hence, it is crucial to understand the objectives that you are trying to achieve through the step-wise procedure you are following.

Without the knowledge of the type of data, you cannot perform exploratory data analysis, and similarly, without a clean dataset, you cannot train an inappropriate model with wrong data and information.

Firstly, it is essential to collect the most useful data that you have available to you and then proceed to explore the data accurately. Once you finish exploring the data, you will have a brief idea of how you can manipulate the data accordingly.

Once you have your cleansed dataset, you can proceed to construct your model and train the dataset accordingly, and choose to make the appropriate predictions during the evaluation step. Mixing these steps up might not be the best idea, and hence it is crucial to a desirable sequential order.

3. Losing Motivation Midway:

Working on numerous AI and Data Science projects can sometimes be a herculean task. While trying to compile a complex code for the first time, the program running will cause the users to run into several issues. You are bound to face these problems on a constant basis in AI and Data Science. While working on these issues, the need for effective debugging is a must.

However, it is not always an easy fix to solve these errors or bugs, especially when the most optimal method is not utilized. Extensive research, dedication, and the right frame of mind are major requirements for approaching these tasks.

When you don’t receive the optimal and appropriate results that you are looking for, it is often easy to notice that some people lose motivation midway.

This factor is one of the primary causes for the failure of most of your AI and Data Science projects. Despite trying your hardest to fix the errors and obtain an optimal solution, you may not be able to interpret it as desired, leading to a frustrating experience.

To fix these issues, the best approach is to ensure that you take sufficient breaks during your work times to keep your calm and composure. It is always best to approach each individual problem one at a time and try to research them on Stack Overflow or GitHub for referring to solutions and answers relating to a similar question.

More often than not, you should be able to find reliable answers to the problems you are struggling at with ease. 4. Resource Constraints:

The worst part about a brilliant idea which you know you can make it work but unfortunately you don’t have the right resources available to you.

For numerous complex Data Science projects, you will either need a large number of data requirements for a collection of large datasets for machine learning tasks.

Or you might require more computational resources such as RAM size or Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) to solve specific tasks on deep learning.

While there are several free resources available for your to construct beginner to intermediate level projects, including cloud platforms, they are rarely sufficient for high-level, complex applications.

Something like a Google Colab could work decently well for intermediate applications, but the time and resource constraints could cause several issues.

When you trying to construct a specific project on something more complex like a project on Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and you need to work with high-resolution images, the resource constraints are a huge issue.

Without proper computational resources, you will always receive a resource exhausted error, or you simply can’t execute your programs.

The potential fixes for these issues are to reduce the image quality and the number of layers in your neural network. While the results might not be as desired, you will end up with some decent results to verify your methodologies and algorithms.

5. Ineffective Collaborative Teamwork:

Data Science and AI is a humungous subject. Sometimes, it is not possible to complete all the projects that you are working on by yourself and requires effective teamwork to succeed.

This point focuses on a group of friends, a team in a start-up or company working together, or other similar group work where the enthusiasts are trying to co-ordinate together to achieve a similar goal.

Oftentimes, when there is no proper coordination and match of ideologies, there could be several ego clashes, disagreements, unnecessary arguments, and other malfunctions, leading to a disastrous experience and ultimately a failure of the project.

Working together as a team is significant because there needs to be a consistent exchange of information on the ongoing project. And hence, this work requires data scientists to work collectively to figure out the best possible solutions, improve the model accuracy, and produce top-quality results during deployment.

To achieve the best results on any type of project, all the members of the team need to be able to share their individual research and works in an efficient way and communicate to the best of their ability. Without a total combined effort, the overall progress of the projects will be hampered.

6. Failure To Adapt And Optimize:

The only thing that will remain consistent about Data Science as you climb the ladders of greatness will be change.

One of the most crucial characteristics to achieve in order to succeed in Data Science and AI is the ability to adapt and optimize effectively.

An algorithm or methodology that is frequently utilized to solve a particular task might be replaced with better technology in the near future.

Most of the transfer learning models that won several competitions in the early 2010s have now been replaced by better transfer learning methodologies to accomplish complex problems.

A similar example for the evolution of object detection algorithms is also a great example of the fast-paced advancement in the field of machine learning and AI.

We went from SIFT and HOG algorithms to more advanced techniques such as YOLO, single-shot detectors, Mask R-CNN, and other similar methodologies to accomplish the best possible results.

Hence, it is significant to keep yourself updated on all the new emerging technologies that arrive in the world of Artificial Intelligence. For this purpose, keep yourself up to date on latest topics with the help of blogs, research papers, and videos.

Data Science and AI are two fields where constant learning and effort are required. The entire experience is a fun and wonderful journey to achieve great results.
